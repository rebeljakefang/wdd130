Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote white water rafting trips by providing clear information about trip options, safety, pricing, and what to expect, so visitors feel confident booking an adventure.

Audience

My target audience is adults and families (ages ~16–60) who want an outdoor adventure. They want to quickly compare trip difficulty levels, see photos, understand what to bring, and book from a phone or laptop.
